phrase that is used to represent a phone number. The word COMPUTE equates to 266-7883
in the associated alpha touchpad indicators. Some businesses, such as an automobile
dealer, in a common exchange might only seek the last four numbers, such as 325-CARS,
instead of 325-2277, for ease of potential customers' memory.
